Bryan Stevenson

Fighting for Justice and Mercy in America's Legal System

Updated 2025 By Furkan

Bryan Stevenson has dedicated his life to fighting for justice and mercy in the American legal system. As a lawyer, author, and social justice advocate, he has worked tirelessly to challenge racial discrimination in the criminal justice system and defend those most desperate for effective legal representation.

"The opposite of poverty is not wealth. The opposite of poverty is justice."

Bryan Stevenson

Early Life and Education

Growing up in southern Delaware during the tail end of segregation, Bryan Stevenson experienced firsthand the lingering effects of racial inequality. His journey through Harvard Law School and early work with the Southern Center for Human Rights shaped his commitment to justice reform.
